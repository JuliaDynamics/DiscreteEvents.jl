Process startup

Under some environments (e.g. Jupyter) it may happen, that the processes have not started completely before the clock runs. In such cases it may help to put a little sleep, e.g. sleep!(0.1) between process!(‚Ä¶) and run!(‚Ä¶) to ensure that all started processes have enqueued for clock events.
